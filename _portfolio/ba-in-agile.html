Role of Business Analyst in Agile setting is not always clearly defined.

To the extent that in some Agile projects there may be even no need of specific person assigned an Analyst role.

In other projects where the Analyst is still a central figure, the whole approach to analysis process and ownership may be changing, which would also require additional set of BA skills.
