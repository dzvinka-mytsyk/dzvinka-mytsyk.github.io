Sprint Zero is a popular concept which some Scrum Teams utilise.

Basically, it is an additional Sprint before the official start of the Project in Sprint #1.

In this presentation I am discovering what are the important elements of Sprint Zero, sharing checklists and tips on how to introduce it in the organization.
