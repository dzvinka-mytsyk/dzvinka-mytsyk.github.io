A little About Me

My name is Dzvinka, and I am Agile Product Owner with 15+ years of experience in software development. I am originally from Ukraine, graduated from one of the best universities in the country – National University of “Kyiv-Mohyla Academy”. Since then I have been working in IT industry in Ukraine, Hungary and Germany.

My background is mostly in back-end projects for big corporate customers in the following domains:

  Data Management

  Natural language processing

  Search optimization

  B2B platform & digital marketplace

  Diital Privacy

  Efficent API Design

I have 7+ years of experience in successful Agile projects (Scrum and Kanban) as Scrum Master, Product Owner or team member. At the same time I have good understanding of currently popular concenps of Lean Startup, DevOps and Continuous Delivery, Lean UX and Analytics.

On this web-page you can find out about me, download my CV, view my work examples in several categories, check out books I recently read (and would recommend) and online courses I completed.
