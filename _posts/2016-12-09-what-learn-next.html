I was always convinced that Business Analyst or Product Owner working on a software project needs to (on some level) understand the technology and stay on top of related technical trends.

This not only makes it easier to collaborate with your technical colleagues, but also has the same effect as with business stakeholders: communication is way more effective afer you invest the effort to learn ins and outs of their business model, processes and market context.
