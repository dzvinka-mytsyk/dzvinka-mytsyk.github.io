Each month I read a number of really great articles about Product, Management, IT or Communication. I think it would be useful to post links to some of the best articles I found in the last 30 days. Let's start with the month of July.

"30 lessons I learned managing people for the first time" is a checklist of possible pitfalls in management. I especially liked lesson #1: "Don’t set any expectations with new hires, apart from the need for them to learn and ask a ton of questions. In other words, expect them to be curious. Expecting anything more has a high chance of catalyzing impostor syndrome."
